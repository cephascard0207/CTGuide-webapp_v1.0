Web Technology

Master web technology with a focus on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while gaining expertise in PHP and DBMS concepts. Explore ER diagrams, data integrity, authenticity, integrity concepts.

Software Technology

Delve into software technology with a focus on Python, covering its modules, object-oriented and logical programming principles. Gain a solid understanding of networking concepts, types, and physical hardware, enhancing your skills across multiple facets of technology.

Software Application

Unlock your creative potential with software applications like Blender, CorelDRAW, and Photoshop. Whether you're designing 3D models, crafting vector graphics, or editing photos, these tools provide the power and flexibility to bring your artistic visions to life

Information Technology

Enhance your IT skills with Microsoft Office proficiency and a solid understanding of web fundamentals. Learn the basics of HTML and gain insights into how the internet works to effectively manage and create digital content.
